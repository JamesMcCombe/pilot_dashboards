Foundations

Two Statements We Ask the FMA to Endorse

A SupTech pilot begins with two simple but powerful points of agreement about offshore leakage and the need for better behavioural insight.

03
Statement 1
NZ retail traders are leaking into unregulated offshore trading funnels.
Statement 2
Insight into their behaviour is required to design effective harm-reduction policy.

These statements do not create new regulation — they define a research and SupTech exploration mandate. Everything else flows from agreement here.
