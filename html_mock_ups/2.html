Problem

The Core Problem

NZ retail traders are being influenced into high-risk offshore trading environments, but regulators lack behavioural insight into what happens next.

02
Current reality
  • Social media funnels NZ traders into offshore prop firms and brokers.
  • Harmful trading often occurs entirely outside the NZ regulatory perimeter.
  • Existing reporting frameworks provide little or no behavioural detail.
  • FMA typically sees complaints and outcomes, not the behavioural journey.
Why this matters
  • Most high-risk behaviour now occurs where the FMA has the least visibility.
  • Without behavioural data, it is difficult to design targeted harm-reduction tools.
  • Traditional conduct and disclosure reporting was not built for social-driven, offshore funnels.

This slide frames the blind spot we are addressing: offshore, socially-driven harm that falls outside conventional broker reporting and surveillance.
